Terms and Terminology

Understanding key terms related to banking and finance.

  • Interest Rate

    The percentage of a sum of money charged for its use, typically annually.

  • Loan

    A sum of money that is borrowed and is expected to be paid back with interest.

  • Credit Score

    A numerical representation of a person's creditworthiness, based on credit history.

  • Mortgage

    A loan specifically for purchasing property, typically repaid over many years.

  • Debit Card

    A card issued by a bank that allows the holder to transfer money electronically from their bank account when making a purchase.

  • Deposit

    A sum of money placed or kept in a bank account, usually to gain interest.
